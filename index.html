Who Are We?

TJ's largest mentorship outreach program that identifies and supports high-achieving middle schoolers of minority backgrounds in order to prepare them for rigorous high school academics. We serve our mentees through tutoring sessions on Saturdays at TJ, one-on-one mentoring, and STEM enrichment opportunities.

"TJHSST LIFT has changed the lives of hundres of aspiring thinkers. I say with pride that we, as a community are making changed"

Jay Wickliff, Coordinator
